Black Mesa (2022)

Source Engine

Lead Level Designer (2019-2022) Level Designer (2013-2019)

Black Mesa is a fan reimagining of the original Half-Life, developed by Crowbar Collective. It modernises the classic experience with updated visuals, gameplay improvements, and a full reworking of the Xen chapters.

I firstly worked on Black Mesa as a Level Designer, and then later as the Lead Level Designer for Xen development and beyond. I contributed two multiplayer levels and numerous levels within the Xen chapters, as well as a vast amount of improvements and bug-fixes to the earlier Earthbound levels. I also served as the Community Manager for several years before that was a dedicated role.
