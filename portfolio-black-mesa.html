Black Mesa

Released 2022 · Source Engine

Lead Level Designer: 2022–2019

Level Designer: 2019–2013

Black Mesa is a modern reimagining of the original Half-Life, developed by Crowbar Collective. It updates the classic game with improved visuals, expanded environmental storytelling, gameplay refinements, and a complete reworking of the Xen chapters.

I worked on Black Mesa across multiple stages of development, first as a Level Designer and later as Lead Level Designer for Xen and subsequent updates. My work included two multiplayer maps, numerous Xen spaces, and a wide range of improvements and bug-fixes across the earlier Earthbound chapters.

Alongside level design, I helped shape the overall player experience through iteration, polish, and long-term support work across the project. I also served as Community Manager for several years before that became a dedicated role.
