Rogue Point

2026

  • Lead Level Designer: 2026–2022
  • Programmer: 2026–2022
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 4.27

Rogue Point is a tactical rogue-lite shooter, and the first original game from indie studio Crowbar Collective, published by Team17.

I wore many hats while working on Rogue Point. I managed the design department as the Lead Level Designer, but also handled a lot of the level design work myself too, contributing 2 of 4 levels for the Early Access release. I also worked alongside our Game Designer on a significant amount of the core gameplay and systems, as we worked to piece together Rogue Point's identity.

I also worked as a programmer too, functioning as a bridge between code and design. I used a combination of C++ and blueprints to build all of Rogue Point's level-based systems and actors, as well as a substantial number of editor utilities and tools to make life easier for designers. I also contributed a significant number of bugfixes and features to the core game as well.
