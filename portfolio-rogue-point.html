Rogue Point

Released 2026 · Unreal Engine 4.27

Lead Level Designer: 2026–2022

Programmer: 2026–2022

Rogue Point is a tactical rogue-lite shooter, and the first original game from indie studio Crowbar Collective, published by Team17.

I wore many hats while working on Rogue Point. I managed the design department as the Lead Level Designer, but also handled a lot of the level design work myself too, contributing 2 levels to the 4 level Early Access release. I also worked alongside our Game Designer on a significant amount of the core gameplay and systems.

I also worked with the code department, serving as a programmer myself, and functioning as a bridge between code and design. I built all of Rogue Point's level-based systems and actors.
